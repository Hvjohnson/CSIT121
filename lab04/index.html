Guacamole

Citrus infused goodness

You will need the following:

  • 2 Avocados
  • Half tablespoon of Lime juice
  • Half tablespoon of orange juice
  • Half tablespoon of pineapple juice
  • Chopped Cilantro
  1. Mix the avocados, lime, pineapple and orange juice in a large bowl.
  2. Mix in the cilantro.
  3. Place an avocado pit into the bowl to keep the guac fresh.

This is my favorite recipe because I love adding cilantro to guac.
