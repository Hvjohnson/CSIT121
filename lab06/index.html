Bodies Bodies Bodies

An A24 Production
Directed by Halina Rejin

Starring:

  • Amandala Stenburg
  • Maria Bakalova
  • Myha'la Herrold
  • Chase Sui Wonders
  • Rachel Sennott
  • Lee Pace
  • Pete Davidson

Bee, a working class Eastern European woman tags along with her wealthy girlfriend Sophie, to a hurricane party held at her friend David's mansion; accompanied by her other very wealthy friends.

As the hurricane begins, the groups decided to play a murder mystery game: Bodies Bodies Bodies, which turns into a real life murder mystery as the night goes on. The movie has a very specific kind of humour that best relates to the Gen Z audience.

It does a good job at portraying the insecurities and petty rivalries of young 20-somethings as they are tested with the suspense and gore that the night brings them.
